# Patient User Guide

This guide is for people using the wearable device.

You do not need technical experience to use this system.

---

## What This Device Does

This wearable device helps you:
- Remember important things
- Get reminders throughout the day
- Contact a caregiver in an emergency

You can talk to the device using your voice.

---

## Turning the Device On

1. Press and hold the side button
2. Wait for the light or sound
3. The device is ready when it speaks or vibrates

---

## Talking to the Device

You can say things like:

- “What is my name?”
- “What am I doing today?”
- “Remind me to take my medication.”

Speak slowly and clearly.

---

## Receiving Reminders

When it’s time for a reminder:
- The device will speak
- Or vibrate
- Or show a message

Just listen. no action is required.

---

## Emergency Help

If you feel unsafe:
1. Press the emergency button
2. The device will contact your caregiver
3. Help will be notified

---

## Charging the Device

- Plug the charger into the wall
- Place the device on the charger
- A light or sound confirms charging.

Charge once per day.
