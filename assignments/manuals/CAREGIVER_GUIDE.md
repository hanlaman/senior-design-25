# Caregiver User Guide

This guide is for caregivers managing the wearable system and companion app.

---

## What Caregivers Can Do

As a caregiver, you can:
- Set reminders
- View schedules
- Receive emergency alerts
- Manage patient information

---

## Initial Setup

1. Install the Companion App
2. Create a caregiver account
3. Pair the wearable device
4. Assign the device to a patient

---

## Managing Reminders

To create a reminder:
1. Open the app
2. Tap **Add Reminder**
3. Enter time and message
4. Save

Examples:
- Medication reminders
- Appointments
- Daily routines

---

## Alerts & Monitoring

You may receive:
- Emergency alerts
- Missed reminder notifications
- Device disconnect alerts

Alerts include:
- Time
- Location (if enabled)
- Alert type

---

## Settings & Preferences

Adjust:
- Voice volume
- Reminder frequency
- Emergency contacts
- Notification style

---

## Troubleshooting

**Wearable not connecting**
- Restart Bluetooth
- Restart app
- Ensure device is charged

**No alerts received**
- Check notification permissions
- Confirm emergency contact info
